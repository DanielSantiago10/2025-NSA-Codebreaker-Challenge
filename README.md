## Overview

The NSA Codebreaker Challenge is a nationally recognized, annual cybersecurity competition designed to simulate real-world intelligence and cyber defense scenarios. Participants are tasked with solving a series of increasingly complex, sequential challenges, each unlocking only after the previous task is completed.

Challenges are scored based on difficulty, with teams ranked by the total points earned across all completed tasks. Solutions may be submitted at any point during the competition window, but success requires persistence, adaptability, and a strong analytical mindset.

Each task calls on a wide range of skills, including technical analysis, logical reasoning, intuition, and common sense. Rather than focusing on isolated tools, the challenge emphasizes thinking like a cyber analyst and learning how to operate in uncertain, high-stakes environments.
